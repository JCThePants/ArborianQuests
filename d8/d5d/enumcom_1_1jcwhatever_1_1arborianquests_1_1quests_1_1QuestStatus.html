Member Function Documentation

QuestCompletionStatus getCompletionStatus ( )

Get the completion status of the quest status.

The completion status specifies if the quest status indicates that the player has completed the quest at least once (QuestCompletionStatus#COMPLETED) or the player has never completed the quest (QuestCompletionStatus#NOT_COMPLETED).

CurrentQuestStatus getCurrentStatus ( )

Get the current quest status of the quest status.

The current quest status specifies if the quest status indicates that the player is at the current moment playing the quest (CurrentQuestStatus#IN_PROGRESS) or that at the current moment the player is not playing the quest (CurrentQuestStatus#NONE).
